world humanity revolution (UK) is a registered British political party for Great Britain — England, Scotland and Wales — dedicated to dignity, fairness and service. We welcome everyone who believes in justice, compassion and practical action.

about world humanity revolution (uk)

Our purpose is simple: protect the sanctity of life, uphold the truth of life, and fulfil the rights of life. We seek clean public service, accountable institutions and community-first solutions across Great Britain.

people & dignity

Policies that prioritise health, education, housing and support for families and carers, so every person can live with dignity.

clean governance

Transparency, anti‑corruption standards and service‑driven leadership in public life.

safe & thriving communities

Neighbourhood‑level investment, green spaces, and practical safety so people can flourish together.

promoter — allama imam hayat

Allama Imam Hayat is recognised by supporters as a voice for truth, service and harmony. His advocacy inspires the movement’s focus on life, compassion and responsible leadership.

about

Allama Imam Hayat has long promoted the ideal that life is sacred and leadership is a trust. His message: build a world of love and life through knowledge, fairness and service to others.
