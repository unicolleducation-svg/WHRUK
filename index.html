About World humanity revolution, UK

The World Humanity Revolution, UK is committed to revolutionising political thought based on humanitarian philosophy. We aim to promote social justice, equality, sustainable development, and human empowerment through nonviolent and inclusive means.

Promoter — Allama Imam Hayat

Promoter

Allama Imam Hayat

A political party and philosophy based on authority of every life irrespective of faith without any materialistic discrimination and inequalities, and humanitarian state & world free from the tyranny of any single group—within a framework of a state structure of free life & a world order of free humanity, grounded in the full natural identity & rights of life
— Allama Imam Hayat

World humanity revolution means

  1. Human Entity. Belief in the existence and human entity above all matters—such as language, ethnicity, state, gender, race, borders—but solely in the name of the kind Creator of life; to believe in the truth of life and live an enlightened humanitarian life for the welfare of all mankind guided by the light of holy Resalat of the Creator.
  2. Humanitarian Sensibility – Humanitarian Virtues – Humanitarian Sight. Human consciousness—mind, support or opposition—is led by some form of consciousness. Imanic consciousness represents the highest realisation of truth and is inseparable from humanitarian consciousness and sights. Both stand in opposition to materialistic consciousness and to extremism in the name of religion or any ideology that denies life and equal security, rights, and dignity. Humanitarian consciousness means recognising the rights given by the Creator and His Rasul to all people and acknowledging the duty to safeguard and serve humanity.
  3. Ownership of Life & Collective Ownership of the World – Khilafat‑e‑Insaniyat. The original owner of life and the universe is Allah and His Rasul. Every human being is given ownership of their own life—free from domination and enslavement—and a share in the collective ownership of the state, wealth and the world: the Khilafat‑e‑Insaniyat.
  4. Every Individual is Humanity Revolution. Each life stands in the stream of truth against falsehood, tyranny and oppression. Humanity Revolution is an existential commitment entrusted by the holy Resalat—never to aid oppression nor surrender to anti‑human forces.
  5. Human Nationality. Restoring a united human identity based on love for all creation through love for the Creator, rescuing humanity from materialistic nationalism that breeds division and exploitation.
  6. Politics of Humanity. Representative, protective and liberating politics that secures everyone’s rights given by Allah and His Rasul—against barbaric, communal and authoritarian politics that deny those rights.
  7. State of Humanity. A universal, inclusive and humanitarian state—free from the tyranny of a single group—grounded in life, collective ownership, security, freedom and rights for all.
  8. World System and Government of Humanity. Toward a natural, universal world of humanity—Khilafat‑e‑Insaniyat—an undivided world of independent lives, beyond the shackles of narrow materialistic borders.
  9. Economy of Humanity. Collective ownership of natural resources; a duty of the state to ensure food, shelter, healthcare, education and a healthy environment for every person; and a democratised flow of life and wealth.

— Allama Imam Hayat
